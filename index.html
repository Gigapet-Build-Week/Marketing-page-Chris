- Who is your target audience? Be specific. Families with children between 5 and 13 years old - What feedback have you gotten from potential users? The pet needs to have different states, a static image will not be engaging. Simple options are better than precise, detailed entries for children. - Have you validated the problem and your solution with your target audience? How? Consulted with an accredited teacher who holds a masters in special education.

- What problem does your app solve? Encouraging children to have healthier, balanced eating habits - Be as specific as possible; how does your app solve the problem? Eating habits and their consequences are hard for anyone to grasp, but especially children. Children need feedback to keep them engaged and by having a pet that responds directly and visibly to their choices we achieve that engagement. - What is the mission statement? Healthier, happier children through engaging feedback.
