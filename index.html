This app is created with Families with children between 5 and 13 years old in mind. We have added features for the pet to have different states, to be more engaging for children. A User Interface with ease in use for parents and their young ones. Let your children watch his/her pet grow while learning to eat healthier and logging it.

-Use the Gigapet app to encourage children to have healthier, balanced eating habits - Parents can login in and allow their childrento add the food they ate throughout the day, if it was a healthy choice your pet becomes happier and can grow, if it a not so healthy choice, your pet can get sick. Eating habits and their consequences are hard for anyone to grasp, but especially children. Children need feedback to keep them engaged and by having a pet that responds directly and visibly to their choices we achieve that engagement. - Our mission is to help promote Healthier, happier children through engaging feedback.

- Some of the features included are an onboarding process for the parent. The Ability to create a food entry for parent or for child usage. The Ability to edit and delete entries made by child or parent. A Display page of daily/weekly/monthly food intake based on category. Not to mention the cute pet that grows with your childs healthy choices.

Using frameworks such as - Express, SQLite3, PostgreSQL, Knex, and Jest for our backend, our team was able to create advanced tracking of metrics such as weight, gender, age. Along with More sophisticated versions tailored for different age groups (e.g. teenagers). Allowing for a wider audience range and a more convenient user experience.
